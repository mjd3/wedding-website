Travel


Deer Park Villa
The wedding ceremony and reception will be taking place at Deer Park Villa in Fairfax, CA, part of beautiful Marin County.

Deer Park Villa includes a lovely redwood grove where the ceremony will be held that evokes memories of our many hiking and backpacking trips together. There are both indoor and outdoor bars and we will have dinner outside under the shade of the trees. We're really excited for everyone to experience the venue with us!

Arriving to Fairfax
If arriving by air, fly into San Francisco International Airport (SFO) or Oakland International Airport (OAK) - Fairfax is about a 1 hour drive from SFO and a 45 minute drive from OAK.

Booking Rooms
For those of you wondering where to stay, we recommend staying at the Embassy Suites in San Rafael or at one of the many Airbnb rentals in the Fairfax or San Rafael area. There will be a shuttle transporting guests to and from the Embassy Suites hotel to the venue.
