places to

Explore


China Camp State Park

This underrated state park features beautiful shoreline views, and has trails ideal for biking in addition to hiking.



Mt. Tamalpais Watershed

Within ten minutes of Deer Park Villa, you'll find some amazing trails and picnic areas in the Mt. Tamalpais Watershed - from lakes to forests to meadows, you can't go wrong!



Samuel P. Taylor State Park

If you're looking for dense redwood groves, definitely check out Samuel P. Taylor Park. Depending on what terrain you'd like to explore, you can enjoy a walk through the trees along a creek, or hike to the top of Barnabe Peak for expansive views!





food to

Eat


Barefoot Cafe

This gem in Fairfax serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner - we love their extensive egg menu!



Fairfax Scoop

For a sweet treat, you must stop by this ice cream spot - don't let the line deter you, it is absolutely worth the wait! Some of our favorite flavors are Honey Vanilla Lavender and Cardamom, along with anything chocolate!



The Hummingbird

If you want to spice things up, or if like Katie you're always on the hunt for beignets, check out this Cajun restaurant just a few minutes from Deer Park Villa.
