What should I wear? What’s the weather like?

We recommend cocktail attire. Our ceremony and dinner will be outdoors with dancing inside, so it might be helpful to bring a light scarf or jacket. For context, these are the historical averages for June 4th in Fairfax, CA:

High of 77°F / 25°C
Low of 51°F / 10°C


I hear this wedding is in the redwoods…do I need to bring hiking boots?

Although we’ll be surrounded by all of nature’s beauty in the Deer Park Villa redwood grove, no one will need to hike. The venue is fully accessible with plenty of comfortable onsite facilities to make you forget you’re in the forest.


What’s the parking situation?

For those staying at the hotels we recommend, we will be offering a shuttle service to and from the venue – check out our “Travel and Accommodations” tab for more information on this option. If you are driving yourself to the venue, there is limited parking onsite, and you can even leave your car overnight as long as you pick it up by 10 AM Sunday morning (just in case the designated driver becomes a little less designated).


Can I take pictures and post them on social media?

We’d love to see all your photos taken throughout the reception, but we are asking for an unplugged ceremony – our photographer and videographer will take care of capturing those memories! If you do want to post reception photos to social media, we’ll be sharing a hashtag so we can collect all the moments for the day.



I still have questions, what’s the best way to reach you?

Feel free to email us with any questions (or wedding hashtag submissions) at katiemike2022@gmail.com! We also respond to our phones, Facebook messages, and carrier pigeons.
