The Story


Our Beginning

Katie and Mike met as baby freshmen at Princeton pre-orientation in fall of 2012. Although we say we met backpacking, technically we met in the gym auditorium right before we left for our six-day trip to the Catskills. Either way, this was the start of a great adventure where we bonded over a shared love of Cracklin’ Oat Bran trail snacks, the “Born to Run” book, and high school cross-country. We started dating about a month later on the day after September 30, known to Mike as September 31 and to Katie and most of the world as October 1.










Our Journey

Since then, we navigated the highs and lows of college together, from formals to finals, with plenty of Hoagie Haven study breaks. We met each other’s families and extended families – which for Katie including joining the annual tradition of the Thanksgiving Manchester Road Race and for Mike included becoming roommates with Katie’s grandfather “Gagoo” on a trip to Scotland. We started a tradition of backpacking on our anniversary with trips to Harriman State Park, not swayed by the occasional October rainstorm. As we became upperclassmen, we joined Tower where we spent many late nights (early mornings?) with our friends, and became leaders in the same outdoor group that facilitated our meet-cute (shout-out to Princeton Outdoor Action and the LTC).

After graduating, we both moved to New York where Katie started her MPH and Mike worked at a robotics start-up. Though our time long distance didn’t really start until summer 2017, with Mike living in the East Village and working in Brooklyn while Katie lived in Harlem with her campus in Washington Heights, trips to visit each other meant many hours on the subway – but they were definitely worth it (and thanks @Netflix for releasing the download feature around this time). When Mike started his PhD at Berkeley, we traded Sunday morning burritos for Skype calls. Our year and a half long distance was made sweeter when Mike visited Katie while she was working in both Mexico and South Africa and when Katie visited Mike in California for backpacking and skiing adventures.

In January 2019, we said goodbye to time-zone struggles and flight connection horror stories when Katie moved to Berkeley. We’ve adopted two cacti and a succulent named Gronk, Trinidad James, and Pearl (guess who named which…) and have spent many weekends on the road in our RAV4 Hybrid heading to Tahoe, Yosemite, Humboldt Redwoods, Monterey, and SoCal. When we’re home, we love spending time on our porch and going on long runs together – more specifically, Mike goes on long runs while Katie bikes next to him – he’s still faster.



Our Engagement

Mike proposed on August 22, 2019 around sunset on a trail in Berkeley’s Panoramic Hill overlooking the Bay, with the help of some friends (hi Dinara and Ryan!). We had a minor hiccup when Mike’s phone died right after he told Dinara that he was near a cactus past some stairs, and that the location pin he originally sent was wrong. Huge props to Dinara for making sense out of these instructions and for convincing a skeptical Katie that “her friend Nick” was really close and we just needed to look at one more spot. When we finally found the stairs and Katie rounded the corner past the cactus, she was shocked to find Mike there instead and helpfully asked “What are you doing here?”, to which Mike responded by getting down on one knee and popping the question. Of course Katie said yes!
